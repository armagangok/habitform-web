# HabitForm 3.0.0 Release Notes

## Major Features

### 🌟 Habit Constellation View
Experience your habits in a beautiful, interactive constellation view. See your habit network come alive with dynamic connections and intuitive navigation. This revolutionary visualization makes tracking multiple habits more engaging and visually appealing.

### 🗺️ Habit Map
Visualize your habit network and discover how habits connect. Build chains of related habits and understand the relationships that make your routine stronger. The Habit Map helps you see the bigger picture of your habit formation journey.

### 📊 Enhanced Formation Insights
Get deeper insights into your habit formation probability with:
- Historical probability tracking with interactive charts
- Year-over-year progress visualization
- Personalized formation time estimates
- Difficulty-aware probability calculations

### ⭐ Reward Rating System
Rate how you feel after completing each habit. This new feature helps personalize your habit formation experience and provides valuable feedback for improving your routine.

### 🎨 Redesigned Home Experience
- Floating navigation bar with improved accessibility
- Circular habit widgets with progressive color feedback
- Multi-completion support for habits with daily targets
- Enhanced visual feedback and animations

## Improvements

- **Performance**: Optimized habit canvas rendering and detail page loading
- **Onboarding**: Enhanced app features showcase with interactive screenshots
- **UI/UX**: Improved grid visibility, color schemes, and overall design polish
- **Accessibility**: Better contrast and visibility across light and dark themes

## Technical Enhancements

- Refactored habit widget architecture for better maintainability
- Improved state management and provider optimization
- Enhanced widget synchronization for iOS Home Widget
- Better error handling and edge case management
